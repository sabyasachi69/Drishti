The Solution

Introducing DRISHTI.

A unified platform that integrates resources, peers, and AI into one seamless educational experience — built for every learner.

API Integrations

Connect Coursera, edX, Khan Academy, and more through a single intelligent interface.

Peer Collaboration Tools

Subject-specific forums, shared documents, study groups, and real-time progress tracking.

Gamified Learning

Badges, leaderboards, and achievements that turn learning into an engaging, rewarding journey.

AI-Powered Personalization

Machine learning models that adapt course recommendations and study plans to each learner.

Multilingual + Offline Access

Breaking barriers of language and connectivity so every student can learn, everywhere.

How It Works

Built for the modern learner.

DRISHTI's three-layer architecture handles integration, personalization, and collaboration seamlessly.

01

Connect & Aggregate

Students and teachers build unified profiles. DRISHTI pulls content from Coursera, edX, Khan Academy, and more — in one place.

02

Collaborate & Discuss

Join subject-specific forums, form study groups, co-edit documents, and track collective progress on shared dashboards.

03

Learn & Play

Earn badges, climb leaderboards, and receive likes and comments — gamification that keeps every learner motivated and engaged.

04

Personalize & Adapt

Our AI engine analyzes learning patterns and surfaces the right courses, resources, and study plans — uniquely for each user.
